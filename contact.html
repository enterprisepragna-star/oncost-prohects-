Common Questions

What is the minimum bulk order quantity?

Our minimum bulk order quantity is 50 pieces. For smaller quantities, browse the standard product listing and add to cart.

Can I get a GST invoice?

Yes. Include your GSTIN in the enquiry form and we'll generate a GST-compliant e-invoice for your order.

Do you offer custom packaging?

Absolutely. We offer branded boxes, pouches, and carry bags. Mention your packaging preference in the enquiry form.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery is 5–10 working days after order confirmation. Express delivery is available for urgent events at an additional charge.
