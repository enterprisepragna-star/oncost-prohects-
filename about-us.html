Who we are

ONCOST is the gifting arm of Pragna Enterprise, founded in 2025 and headquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana. We started with a simple belief — that a thoughtful gift is the easiest way to make people feel valued. Whether it's a brass diya set for a wedding return-gift, a German-silver gift box for Diwali, or a custom-branded hamper for your company offsite, every piece we ship is hand-picked, quality-checked and lovingly packed.

What we do

Return Gifts

Wedding, birthday, pooja, baby shower, housewarming — pieces guests actually take home and use.

Corporate Gifting

Employee gifts, client hampers, Diwali boxes, conference giveaways — branded, GST-billed, delivered nationwide.

Bulk Orders

50 pieces to 5,000 — custom packaging, your logo or motif, and a dedicated account manager.
